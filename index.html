As a parent on an elementary school child, we know it is darn hard to determine which reading level you child is currently at. Today you need to wait until the techer assesses your child a few times a year to know if they are progresing and keep up. We want to be able to provide you instant feedback on your child's reading level at any time. We can give your child a reading test any time and score it to determine their current reading level. In addtion we can help you find books that are appropriate for your child's current reading level and also stretch them to push them townard the next level. If you find a book online or in a store and wnat to know if it is at the right leve lfor your child we can tell you? All we need is either the ISBN number or name of the book. If it is not in our database yet then we can have you take a picture of a page the book and analyze it to determine the approximate level of the book right on the spot.

For some students, like our child they need more external motivation to read more books more often. With our service you can create custom challenges with you child and have rewards for competing and winning against an instant group of kids. You specify the characteristics of the kids (e.g. boy, likes sports, lies in the county, age 9 - 10) and we can invite kids who match to join in on the reading competition. You and your child can check it to see their progress and how ti compares to their instant group. No matter waht motivates your child we can create achallenge to push them forward to the next level.
